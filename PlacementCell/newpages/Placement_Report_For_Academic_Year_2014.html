Placement Report For Academic Year 2014-2015

This academic year our college has witnessed a good number of corporates visiting our campus. The companies which have came to campus recruitment ranges from Blue-chip companies like Wipro, L & T to the new startups like Redstone Marketing Services. Companies were from diverse sectors like IT, Banking, Insurance, and Marketing Research.

In order to fill up the gap between the skills required by the industry and skills acquired by the normal curriculum, the college under the leadership of Dr. Sangeeta Kohil conducted a programme Campus to Corporate. The aim of this programme was to fill this gap and prepare the students for the corporate world.
